## Design Patterns Used

### Hub-and-Spoke
Town Square acts as the central hub with spokes leading to different zones.

### Progressive Difficulty
Easy ‚Üí Medium ‚Üí Hard zones as you move away from the center.

### Thematic Clustering
Related rooms grouped together (crafting zone, nature zone, residential, etc.).

### Vertical Exploration
UP/DOWN navigation adds depth (Repeater Tower, Underground levels).

### Gated Content
Dark rooms require light sources; final chamber requires crafted key.

### Safe Zones
Multiple safe rooms provide respawn points and rest areas.
